Vincentian Lay Missionaries

Vincentian Lay Missionaries (VLM) is a not-for-profit volunteer sending organisation. Our volunteers work in Ethiopia, Ghana, Kenya and Burkina Faso in partnership with the Daughters of Charity, the Vincentian Fathers, their staff and supporters. All of our partner projects strive to serve the poorest and most marginalised people in their communities. Our volunteers play an important part in that.
