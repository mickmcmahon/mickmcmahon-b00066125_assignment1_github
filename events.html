Miles for Mission

Thanks to all who joined up on Saturday 20th October in Dublin’s Phoenix Park to run, walk or jog miles for mission! This year’s chosen project was Atse Primary School, a Daughters of Charity School, in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. More than €2,600 was raised at the event, which was co-hosted by World Missions Ireland, www.wmi.ie, Pope Francis’ official charity for overseas mission. A big thanks to those who helped out on the day and all who contributed generously through sponsoring runners and supporting the event.
